## Conversion system on `/2026`
- **Primary verb everywhere:** *Get tickets* / *Compra boletos* (oxblood), one visual weight above all else.
- **Sticky mobile bar** on this page throughout: `from $79 · Aug 8 · [Get tickets]`.
- **Countdown:** permitted here, *small and honest*, framed as anticipation ("The oaks are waiting"); switches to day-of wayfinding on Aug 8.
- **Analytics:** `select_tier`, `click_get_tickets` (tier/artist params) as the macro-conversion (checkout is on AXS); reconcile to AXS reports.
- **Retargeting LPs** (`/2026/lp/...` by intent — artist, familia, becas, asientos, última semana, día-del-evento) inherit this page's system and change only the first message.

## Why this split is the whole point
Putting all of this here — and **out of the homepage** — is what lets the homepage be a permanent institution and lets `/2026` be a ruthless, honest conversion page. After August 8, `/2026` is archived to `/legacy/2026` (recap, photos, "who played"), and `/2027` takes its place. The institution never expires; the edition rotates.
