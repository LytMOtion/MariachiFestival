Leadership

The people who carry it forward.

Meet the volunteer Board of Directors of the Santa Barbara Mariachi Festival, Inc.

A dedicated group of volunteers, guided by one purpose: culture, education, and community.

The Santa Barbara Mariachi Festival, Inc. is led by board members who share a common passion — to preserve and embrace our culture, and to promote higher education in the Latinx community of the Central Coast. In true volunteer spirit, they contribute their time, talent, and resources to the festival’s mission.
