Scholarships

Opening doors for the next generation.

Since 1996, one night of mariachi has funded college scholarships for local Latino students.

The scholarships are the reason the festival exists.

Every year, proceeds from the festival and its sponsors fund scholarships for Latino students from Santa Barbara County, awarded in partnership with the Scholarship Foundation of Santa Barbara. Recipients are honored with their families at a reception before the festival — and receive tickets to the show they helped make possible.

How it works

01

The festival raises the funds

Ticket sales, sponsorships, and community support are directed to the scholarship fund.

02

Awarded with a trusted partner

Scholarships are administered with the Scholarship Foundation of Santa Barbara.

03

Students are celebrated

Recipients and families are honored at a reception before the festival each summer.

Where they go

Stanford · Brown · UCLA · UC Santa Barbara · Cal Poly — and universities across California.

How to apply

Scholarships are administered with the Scholarship Foundation of Santa Barbara. Eligibility and timing are set each year — contact the festival for current information.
