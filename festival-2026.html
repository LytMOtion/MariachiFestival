Plan Your Visit

Getting there

Santa Barbara Bowl, 1122 N. Milpas St. Neighborhood parking is limited — arrive early or use rideshare.

Good to know

All ages · outdoor amphitheater (bring a sweater) · mobile entry · limit 10 tickets per order.

FAQ

What time should I arrive?

Gates open at 4:00 PM; the show starts at 5:00. Come early to park and settle in.

Is it family-friendly?

Yes — it is an all-ages event and a multigenerational tradition.

Where does my money go?

Proceeds fund scholarships for local Latino students, awarded with the Scholarship Foundation of Santa Barbara.
